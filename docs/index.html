A fast-paced typing survival game.

 

📚 How to Play

🎯 Objective

Words fall from the top of the screen. Type them before they reach the bottom to survive for 2 minutes and earn your promotion!

⚠️ Rules

  • If a word reaches the bottom, you get a strike.
  • 3 strikes and you're fired!
  • Survive 2 minutes to get promoted.
  • Difficulty increases over time — words fall faster and spawn more frequently.

🎮 Controls

  • Just start typing to match a falling word.
  • Words are matched live as you type — no need to press Enter.
  • Press Esc to pause/resume.

⭐ Scoring

  • Points = word length × 10 × combo multiplier.
  • Streak multipliers: ×1 (0-2), ×1.5 (3-5), ×2 (6-9), ×3 (10+).

📈 Ranks

Your rank climbs as you progress: Intern → Associate → Manager → Director → VP
